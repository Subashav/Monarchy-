The Roadmap

Our Hiring Journey.

We value your time as much as ours. Our process is streamlined, transparent, and designed to find a mutual fit within 14 days.

01

Application Review

We review every application. No bots, no automated filters. We look for proof of execution, curiosity, and alignment with our mission.

02

Cultural Sync

A 30-minute introductory call to understand your ambitions and shared values. We care more about who you are than where you worked.

03

Execution Challenge

A real-world task related to your role. We want to see how you think, solve problems, and document your process under pressure.

04

Final Strategy

Meet the leadership team to discuss your role's impact and our long-term roadmap. If it's a fit, you'll receive an offer within 24 hours.
